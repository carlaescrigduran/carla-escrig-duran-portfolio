About Me

3D Animation Concept Art Visual Dev Storyboarding

I'm Carla, a 3D animator and concept artist based in Spain, currently completing my degree in Animation. Passionate about bridging the gap between storytelling and technology, I specialise in character animation, environment design, and visual development. My work is driven by a love for expressive movement and narrative depth.
